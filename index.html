About Impact Hacks

Impact Hacks is a student-run, all-female high school hackathon based out of the University of California, Berkeley. Attendees are asked to build a software project for social impact in one of three categories: Tech + Health, Tech + Equality, Tech + Earth. We hope to encourage more women to pursue computing fields as well as promote the use of technology for ethical purposes.

Frequently Asked Questions



Where and when is Impact Hacks?

Impact Hacks be held on February 11, 2020. It will be hosted on UC Berkeley campus: Soda Hall Wozniak Lounge

How do I form a team?

You don't need to have a team formed beforehand - you can form teams during the hackathon. Each team can have 2 to 4 individuals.

Do I need any programming experience?

None at all! Beginners are welcome. There will also be workshops where you can improve your skills, mentors throughout the hackathon to help you personally, and you can also learn from other students!

How much will Impact Hacks cost?

This event, including all food, swag, and prizes, is completely free!
